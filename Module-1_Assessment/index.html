About Me

Cheers! I am a full stack developer and a Registered Nurse. I have over 15+ years experience in an acute care setting at a Level 1 trauma center. With my vast and unique medical background, I am interested in combining the "art of nursing" with the skills that I have acquired as a web developer, to create meaningful user/client experiences.

Fun Facts: In my free time, I love to explore the world! Paris, Rome, London, Athens, Belize, Cambodia (just to name a few)... I crave cultural experiences- especially the food! Hiking and camping are probably my most favorite activities. I have a National Parks passport book that, I sadly admit, needs more stamps and stickers... but gloomy days spent watching horror movies or reading a good book are also ways that I like to pass the time. I also enjoy pinball and games (table-top, console, pc), "Catan anyone?"

Odds & Ends: I will say that I enjoy good company, and being adventurous, but I'm actually quite an introvert and maybe because of that, I can come off as having a tepid disposition. However, the hardest lesson I am learning (as more learning experiences come with age) is acceptance of myself- all of my "self". These days I attempt to cherish the times when I am my own good company. The occasions when I can sing off key as loud as I want at home, going to a movie or restaurant alone, the early morning hot cup of coffee while the world around me is still not quite awake, or sometimes just sitting in solitude on a park bench... I have learned that these moments during my day, these times in my life when there are less distractions, are just as important as cultivating relationships and pushing the boundaries of my introverted self.
